Brief Overview of Hardware

The computer hardware is the visible part of a computer system. It comprises of the Input,Output processing and memory parts. The various part work in tandem under the instruction of programs to execute commands.
The Keyboard,trackpad and mouse and ports are examples of input parts, while the screen,printers and ports are examples of output parts. The processor which is the sometimes called the brain of the computer is part of the motherboard embedded within the laptop frame.


The Processor

   The processor is the critical part of the computer. The entire performance of a computer system is most affected by the quality of the processor purchased. Before buying a computing set,it is important to consider the following factors:
  • Clock speed
  • Brand name
  • Efficiency and benchmark score

Another component that is of utmost importance is the Graphic processor . It handles all graphical processing reducing the load the from the main processor.The two main suppliers of such parts are nvidia and AMD.

Memory

The memory comprises of the following:

  1. RAM:which is the random access memory. It is volatile as it is where the processor and fetch and store data while processing.A good ddr5 ram is preferred due to speed and reliability feature of modern manufacturing process.
  2. Secondary Memory:Solid state drive should be preferred as hard drives are now outdated.
